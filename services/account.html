Account Opening
The account opening process is when the customer submits the application form and required information and documents to open an account with the bank.

It is not necessary to visit the bank for an account opening; users can now open the account by providing minimal documents as well as other options available at the Paydeer store.

Making a bank deposit is easy, and one can select the bank you prefer at your convenience and most reputable private and government-owned banks have been partnered with the service of Paydeer, and here you benefit from the best banking facilities and These banks are also able to avail of financial loans and credit at attractive rates.

With Paydeer service, you can open your account in the following banks -

  • Axis Bank
  • SBI (coming soon)
  • Yes Bank

Required Documents Opening Bank Accounts in Paydeer -

  • Identity Proof
  • Voter id
  • Pan card
  • Aadhar card
  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Niwas Praman Patra
  • Email Id
  • Phone Number
